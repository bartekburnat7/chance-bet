How To Play

Place a Bet

Place a preffered bet in the first input field, make sure the amount is larger than £1 and less then or equal to your balance.

Choose Colour

Make a choice of what colour you would like to place a bet on, you can only choose one colour per bet. If you guess the right colour you will be rewarded X4 your bet size.

Good Luck!!!

Once you chose the bet size and your choice of colour, press the "BET" button and let the wheel do the work. Make sure to bet responsibly!
